About me

Hello! My name is Laura Máteffy. I am a full-time student at the Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava, at the Faculty of Civil Engineering. I study civil engineer- ing, architecture, and urbanism. Currently, I am in the fourth year of my bachelor’s studies, during which I spent one semester on an Erasmus+ exchange program in Barcelona. During this time, I had the opportunity to work on several projects. As part of my school assignments, I designed a family house, an apartment build- ing, and proposed changes to the use of historical monuments. I worked on proj- ects involving the reconstruction and modernization of buildings and designed the interior of a Thai restaurant. I also worked on an urban planning study for the city of Doldrecht in the Netherlands and the Vikki university campus in Finland. I also participated in a summer school organized by a technical university in the Nether- lands and workshops by Ugreen, as I am interested in sustainability. Additionally, along- side my studies, I gain practical experience in an architectural studio, where I work on projects from concept design to construction realization of various types of buildings.
